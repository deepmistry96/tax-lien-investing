# Legal Notices in Tax Lien Investing

## Types of Notices
- Initial tax delinquency notice
- Notice of tax sale
- Right to redeem notice
- Foreclosure notice
- Final judgment notice

## Requirements by Stage
1. Pre-Sale Notices
   - Timing requirements
   - Content requirements
   - Delivery methods
   
2. Post-Sale Notices
   - Certificate holder obligations
   - Statutory requirements
   - Proof of service

## Best Practices
- Document all notices sent
- Use certified mail
- Keep delivery receipts
- Follow state guidelines
- Maintain communication logs

## Common Mistakes
- Missing deadlines
- Incorrect addresses
- Incomplete information
- Improper service method
