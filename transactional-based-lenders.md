# Transactional-Based Lenders

## Overview
Aggressive lenders who focus on the specific transaction or deal rather than traditional creditworthiness metrics.

## Key Features
- Deal-specific evaluation
- Quick approval process
- Higher risk tolerance
- Asset-based focus
- Flexible terms

## Evaluation Criteria
- Deal profitability
- Asset value
- Exit strategy
- Market conditions
- [[ROI]] potential

## Common Terms
- Higher interest rates
- Shorter term lengths
- Performance-based pricing
- Quick funding
- Minimal documentation

## Best Uses
- Quick acquisitions
- Auction purchases
- Bridge financing
- Time-sensitive deals
- [[exit-strategies]] alignment

## Risk Factors
- Higher costs
- Strict deadlines
- Performance requirements
- [[bankruptcy-impact]]
- Collateral risks
